# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful and considerate
- Assume positive intent
- Provide constructive feedback

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Trolling, insulting, or demeaning comments
- Sharing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and may remove or edit contributions that violate this code.

## Contact

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer via GitHub issues.
